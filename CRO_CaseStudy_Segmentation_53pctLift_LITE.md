# CRO Case Study: Behavioral Segmentation → +53% Application Lift

---

**Funnel:** Early product selection | **Traffic:** High-intent refinance flow | **Goal:** Increase folder completions

---

## Problem
Users were not clearly guided between refinance vs home equity options, causing misalignment and drop-off downstream.

## Hypothesis
Introduce a segmentation question (current mortgage rate) to guide users toward the most relevant product path, improving decision clarity and downstream conversion.

## Experiment
Added qualifying question after loan purpose: *"What is your current mortgage interest rate?"*
Dynamic routing based on response directed users to refinance or home equity.

**Sample:** 49,827 visitors | **Control:** Standard flow | **Variant:** Segmented experience

---

## Results

| Metric | Lift |
|--------|------|
| Lead Rate | **+8.6%** |
| Chat Transferred | **+16.4%** |
| Folders (Applications) | **+53.1%** |

---

## Key Learning
**Decision clarity and smart segmentation outperform generic product flows.**

One well-placed question reduced cognitive load, increased relevance, and built trust. Lift compounded as users progressed, with the strongest gains at the application stage where product-path misalignment historically caused the most friction.

---

**Takeaway:** Segmentation belongs in the funnel, not just the targeting layer. High-signal questions at critical decision points unlock step-change conversion lifts.
