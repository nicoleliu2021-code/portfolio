# CRO Case Study: Audience-Tailored Experience → +43% Application Lift

---

**Funnel:** Landing page | **Traffic:** VA refinance/purchase segment | **Goal:** Increase application starts

---

## Problem
Generic mortgage experience did not reflect the specific identity or needs of VA loan-eligible users, missing an opportunity to build immediate trust and relevance.

## Hypothesis
Tailor headline and visual treatment to reflect the VA audience directly, improving engagement and conversion through increased relevance and credibility.

## Experiment
Tested VA-specific headline + hero image vs generic experience.

**Control:** Generic mortgage messaging and visuals
**Variant:** VA-specific headline and military/veteran hero image

---

## Results

| Metric | Lift |
|--------|------|
| Visit-to-Lead | **+5.0%** |
| Visit-to-Application | **+43.3%** |

---

## Key Learning
**Audience relevance compounds when copy and visuals work together, not independently.**

Tailoring both elements around the same identity created a multiplicative effect. The stronger lift at application (vs lead) shows that audience-specific experiences reduce abandonment at high-intent decision points, where trust and relevance matter most.

---

**Takeaway:** For high-value audience segments with distinct identities, tailored experiences are conversion multipliers. When users see themselves reflected in the experience, they convert.
